Game Objects

The games in the Trine series are fantasy platformers developed by Finnish company FrozenByte and set in medieval times. They require the player to think logically when overcoming obstacles or enemies, as the three key characters in the game have different skills and attributes. " The Trine " is the first game in the series and is the focus of this website. You can find out more, Dear Reader, below. Enjoy your reading

Przedmioty

During an adventure full of dangers and logical puzzles, numerous items that increase skills or vital parameters are invaluable support. Here are the items and skills we can obtain:

The Trine is a magical item responsible for spiritual harmony and righteousness of heart, which we acquire at the beginning of the game... or rather it acquires us - and gives us the ability to switch between a wizard, a thief and a knight, depending on whose skills we value more and/or which of them are needed in a given situation. The Trine is the only one of the Three Artifacts that was not stolen after the death of the Artifact Guardians.

Amulet of Sacrifice is an item that allows its owner to draw missing energy from (required) health.

Enchanted Crystal of Health - increases the potency of the Health Vial and any other health elixir by 5%.

Vial of Health automatically restores 25% of the owner's health if their health is less. Can be used again at a checkpoint.

Amulet of Kings increases the wielder's maximum health by 15% and can be used in conjunction with other health-increasing items.

Amulet of Stamina increases the maximum health of the character that wields it by 10%.

Guardian's Necklace - is an ancient item that increases maximal health by 10%.

Jewel of Resurrection allows a fallen character who owns it to resurrect once per level.

Fish Scales - allow the owner to spend any amount of time underwater without worrying about losing their health.

Ring of Sorcery slightly increases the owner's total energy.

Amulet of Energy Absorption draws energy from the undead and other creatures (monster kill +1 energy); restores energy to inactive owner under the protection of the Trine.

Energy Vial restores 30% of the owner's energy when it reaches zero. Once used, it is available at the next checkpoint.

Enchanted Energy Crystal enhances the effects of all energy potions by 5%.

Eagle Statuette increases the wizard's magical power, while reducing energy consumption when casting spells such as "Levitation".

Power Necklace allows you to draw more energy from the environment and increases enemy damage from your weapon by 25%.

Light Prism increases both the health and energy of the character that wields it by 25% at each checkpoint.

Energy Ball increases the character's maximum energy by 10% and can be used with other energy items.

Bottle of poison allows you to deal more damage to enemies when you dip a sword blade or an arrowhead in it.

Leggings slightly reduce the damage the hero takes from enemy attacks on the legs.

Bracers partially reduce the damage received from attacks that the opponent launches against the owner's hands.

Wolfgang's Box - an item that allows the wizard to control 2 more chests conjured by him; also for use with the Blue Masterstone.

Blue Masters' Stone increases the maximum possible number of wizard chests and arrows for the thief's regular bow by 1.

Victory Crown increases the number of bridges the wizard can control and conjure by 2.

Bridgeis a spell that a wizard can cast when help is needed to cross obstacles, chasms, or even to crush opponents with a conjured bridge. This spell allows the size of an object to be adjusted.

Levitating Platform is a new wizard skill, useful in situations where you need to get somewhere very high, having a stable support in the air. The next levels of development of the spell mean more possible platforms.

Fire Bowallows the thief to destroy various objects, including those conjured by Amadeus the Magnificent, as well as light torches or simply deal heavy damage to enemies from a distance.

Quick Shot - the thief is able to draw the bow string faster and faster (as skill levels are added through experience points), until it draws almost instantly.

The Power of the Titansenables the knight to pick up and throw small objects; after developing the skill with experience points, he can successively pick up and throw heavy objects and charge (best against the strongest opponents).

Hammer of Stormsis a double-sided weapon blessed by the gods themselves, which destroys basically everything in its path. The next level of weapon development allows its power to combine with the power of the elements of air and earth, which makes the earth tremble from it, and a powerful shock wave annihilates every monster in a radius of 8 meters. The knight becomes a true executioner of the forces of darkness!

Other items

In addition to the items and artifacts listed above, the game also features energy or health elixirs, as well as experience points left behind by killed enemies. Health or energy points are only awarded to the character who collects them. Experience points, on the other hand, are shared. In addition, each level of the game has at least two checkpoints (at the beginning and end), which are luminous balls resting on columns.
