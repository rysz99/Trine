Characters

In the game we meet three main characters on whom the action is based. These characters are:

Zoya The Thief - a thief, equipped with a bow and arrows and a rope with a hook. Excellent in her profession, as evidenced by the nickname she gained. Her next goal is a treasure hidden in the sanctuary of the Astral Academy, where magicians and warriors have been trained for centuries. The treasure turns out to be one of the magical Artifacts responsible for harmony in the Kingdom - the Triad. It was he who summoned her and connected her soul and the souls of two other heroes into one.

Amadeus the Wizard - wizard and womanizer. Recently he experimented with a potion to create fake fireballs to impress the ladies. The spell failed, and to make matters worse it put him into a 2-week sleep. Now he woke up and saw a powerful enemy army. Concerned for his life he decided to escape and joined the thief, attracted by the light coming from inside the temple. His soul is merged with the souls of the thief and the knight.

Pontius the Knight - a brave and strong knight, who wants to serve the Kingdom and the king in the fight against the undead. He trains in swordsmanship and shield use within the walls of the Academy. He notices the light shining from the temple and runs towards it to chase away potential thieves. He joins Zoya and Amadeus and unites with them spiritually.

The Undead Army are human skeletons that rose from their graves as a result of the theft of two Artifacts, which brought chaos and evil to the Kingdom, which was reflected in magic and nature. They are:

Regular Skeletons - they have swords; very easy to defeat due to their predictable behavior.

Shield Skeletons - much more serious opponents.

Armored Regular Skeletons - in addition to the sword, they are protected by armor. They are twice as difficult to defeat.

Armored Shield Skeletons - even harder to defeat. They can block the attack and attack unexpectedly.

Archer Skeletons - They are excellent archers whose disadvantage is that they have no other weapon and are static.

Armored Archer Skeletons - They can shoot a bow at much greater distances. More difficult for the Thief to hit with an arrow. However, they do not pose any challenge to the Knight.

Firebreather Skeletons - breathing fire from a torch at a distance of about 5-6 meters, which is a serious threat, and the knight's shield does not protect him from damage to a satisfactory degree. It is best to hit these skeletons from a long distance with arrows or quickly jump at them and defeat them with a series of sword cuts. They are slightly less dangerous than other creatures.

Other creatures include not only mutated animals that really live in nature, but also fantastic monsters. These include:

Bats - These creatures aren't vampires, but they still give our heroes goosebumps and even death. Their teeth and wings are a big threat.

Spiders - mutated creatures that we first meet in the Castle. They shoot webs from their mandibles, causing significant damage. At close range, they can bite.

The Glowback - a creature similar to an ogre and a troll at the same time. It is very fast and strong, and its roar is loud and terrifying. The back of this monster protects the crystal, but for the knight and his sword it is a piece of cake.

Big Skeleton - a skeleton-like machine that deals two types of thrusts with a giant sword: from below and from above.

Sarek - the ruler of all evil beings, the embodiment of evil. He is a spirit that takes the form of a skeleton. He can conjure everything that a wizard can, plus objects belonging to dark magic, i.e. various deadly obstacles. He is the one who sends all the monsters to the heroes, and his fall means peace and harmony in the Kingdom.

About enemies

Although this is a fairly obvious thing for players, I'll mention it anyway. Namely, as the difficulty of the game increases, the number of regular enemies at a given stage of the game and the strength, endurance, and speed of bosses' actions increase.
